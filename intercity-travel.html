The long road, reconsidered

The best journeys are the ones you get to enjoy.

There is a version of the road trip everyone remembers fondly: the landscape sliding past, the conversation drifting, the stop for chai at the right roadside spot. And there is the version most people actually experience, white knuckles in motorway traffic, tired eyes at hour four, arriving somewhere important with nothing left to give.

The difference is not the road. It is who's driving. Hand the wheel to a professional and the same five hours become yours again: to work through the quarter's numbers, to watch the Salt Range go by, to sleep until Lahore. Families arrive still speaking to each other. Executives arrive with the presentation rehearsed. Everyone arrives well.

Mandil's intercity service is that better version, made bookable: door-to-door journeys between Pakistan's cities in luxury vehicles, with drivers who treat the motorway as their office, because it is.

Questions, answered

Before you set out.

How far will you travel?+

Anywhere in Pakistan. Lahore and Peshawar are daily runs; Multan, Karachi-bound legs and northern valleys are all bookable. If it has a road, we'll quote it.

How is the fare calculated?+

Per route and vehicle class, fixed before departure. Fuel, tolls and the driver are included, the quote is the total.

Can we stop along the way?+

Of course, meals, prayers, photographs, chai. Reasonable stops are part of the journey, not extras. Your driver also knows the best ones.

Do you do round trips?+

Yes, same-day returns, or multi-day trips where the car and driver stay with you at your destination. Both are quoted clearly in advance.

Is one-way travel available?+

Yes, one-way door-to-door is standard, no return obligations, no drop-off penalties.

Is it suitable for family travel?+

It's built for it, seven-seat SUVs, patient professional drivers, and journey pacing that respects children and elders alike.

Do you provide child seats?+

Yes, on request at booking, tell us ages and we'll have appropriate seats fitted before pickup.

How much luggage can we bring?+

SUVs carry five or more large cases; sedans three. Wedding season loads and extra cargo? Mention it and we'll size the vehicle accordingly.

Can companies book intercity travel on account?+

Yes, team movements, site visits and executive runs consolidate onto monthly invoicing through a corporate account.

Can we bring a pet?+

Small pets in carriers are welcome with prior notice, so we assign a suitable vehicle and prepare it properly for the next guest too.

Which vehicles are available?+

Land Cruiser, Prado, Fortuner and Tank 500 for space and rough roads; Mercedes E-Class, BMW 5 Series and Civic for motorway routes; group vehicles for larger parties.

How do you keep long journeys safe?+

Rested drivers on rotation rules, pre-departure vehicle checks, weather-aware routing and a base team monitoring every journey until arrival.

What about night travel?+

Overnight and late departures are available on motorway routes with our most experienced drivers. For mountain roads we schedule daylight driving, comfort includes judgement.

How do I pay?+

Cash, bank transfer or card, before or on the day, as agreed at booking. Corporate accounts settle monthly.

What is the cancellation policy?+

Free with reasonable notice; terms stated on your confirmation. Postponing to another date is always the easy option.

What if plans change mid-journey?+

Routes and destinations can flex on the road, agree the change with your driver and base, and the fare adjusts transparently.

What happens in case of breakdown or emergency?+

Our 24/7 line coordinates assistance and, where needed, a replacement vehicle to complete your journey. You are never stranded on our watch.

Can you collect us from the airport for an intercity run?+

Seamlessly, flight-tracked pickup at Islamabad International, then straight onto the motorway. One booking, one car, zero friction. See airport transfers.

How far ahead should we book?+

A few days for choice of vehicle; more around Eid, wedding season and summer weekends. Same-day journeys are often possible, ask.

Why not just fly or take the train?+

Door-to-door often wins on total time once airports and stations are counted, and always wins on comfort, luggage freedom and leaving when you please. It's travel without the in-between.
