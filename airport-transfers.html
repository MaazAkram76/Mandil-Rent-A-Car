After the flight

The journey should get easier the moment you land.

Every traveller knows the feeling. The seatbelt sign switches off, the cabin lights lift, and somewhere between the aisle and the baggage carousel, the calm of the flight gives way to a quiet uncertainty. Will the car be there? Will the driver know the terminal? Will the last mile of a five-thousand-mile journey be the one that undoes it?

For the executive stepping off a red-eye into a morning of meetings, for the family arriving with tired children and a week of luggage, for the guest visiting Pakistan for the very first time, the pickup is not a detail. It is the difference between arriving and simply landing.

This is why Mandil exists. We built our airport service around a single conviction: the most valuable thing we can hand you at arrivals is certainty. A tracked flight. A named welcome. A cool, quiet cabin and a chauffeur who already knows the way. From the jet bridge to your front door, nothing left to chance.

Questions, answered

Everything you might wonder.

How do I book an airport pickup?+

Book online, by WhatsApp or by phone. Share your flight number, arrival terminal and destination address, and you'll receive written confirmation with a fixed fare within minutes.

Where will my chauffeur meet me?+

Inside the arrivals hall, holding a sign with your name. Your chauffeur's photo, phone number and vehicle registration are shared with you before you fly, so you always know who you're looking for.

What happens if my flight is delayed?+

Nothing you need to act on. We track your flight in real time and adjust the pickup automatically, whether you land forty minutes early or four hours late, your chauffeur is there when you walk out.

Is waiting time charged?+

Every airport pickup includes generous complimentary waiting time after landing, enough to clear immigration, collect luggage and pass customs unhurried. Extended waits beyond that are agreed transparently, never sprung on you.

How is the fare calculated?+

Fares are fixed at booking based on vehicle class and destination. There are no meters, no surge pricing and no fuel surcharges, the amount you confirm is the amount you pay.

Which vehicles are available for transfers?+

Executive sedans (Mercedes E-Class, BMW 5 Series, Honda Civic), luxury SUVs (Lexus LX 570, Land Cruiser, Prado, Fortuner) and economical options for solo travellers. See the full fleet and choose at booking.

Do you offer airport drop-offs too?+

Yes, the same service in reverse. Your chauffeur arrives early, loads your luggage and delivers you to departures with comfortable time in hand, timed against live traffic.

Can I book for someone else, a guest or client?+

Absolutely. Many of our transfers are booked by assistants, hotels and companies for arriving guests. The traveller receives the chauffeur details directly, and you receive confirmation the pickup is complete.

Do you provide child seats?+

Yes, on request at booking. Tell us the ages of the children travelling and we'll fit appropriate seats before the pickup at no drama and no delay.

How much luggage can I bring?+

Sedans comfortably take three large cases; our SUVs take five or more. Travelling heavy? Mention it at booking and we'll assign a vehicle with room to spare.

Are late-night and early-morning pickups available?+

We operate around the clock, every day of the year. A 3 am landing receives exactly the same chauffeur, vehicle and welcome as a midday one, at the same fixed fare.

I'm visiting Pakistan for the first time. Will this be easy?+

It's designed for exactly that. Your chauffeur meets you inside the terminal, speaks English, handles your luggage and knows your destination, no local SIM, cash negotiation or navigation required on your part.

How do I pay?+

Cash, bank transfer and major cards are all accepted. Corporate clients can settle by monthly invoice through a corporate account.

Can I change or cancel my booking?+

Plans change, especially around flights. Amendments are free with reasonable notice, and cancellation terms are shared clearly at booking. A delayed or rescheduled flight is never treated as a cancellation.

Is the service safe for solo travellers?+

Yes. Every chauffeur is identity-verified and professionally trained, every journey is logged and trackable, and your booking details are shared only with you. Many solo and female travellers use Mandil for precisely this reason.

Do you serve both domestic and international arrivals?+

Both, at Islamabad International Airport. Tell us your flight number and we'll be at the correct hall, domestic or international, at the correct time.

Can you handle group or delegation arrivals?+

Yes, multi-vehicle pickups are coordinated as a single operation, with one point of contact for the whole group. Common for corporate delegations, wedding parties and touring groups.

What if I can't find my chauffeur?+

You'll have their direct number, and our support line answers 24/7. In practice it rarely happens, your chauffeur is at the arrivals exit with your name before you clear customs.

Do you offer transfers to cities beyond Islamabad?+

Yes. Airport pickups connect directly with our intercity travel service, land in Islamabad, be driven to Lahore, Peshawar, Murree or anywhere else, in the same car with the same chauffeur.

What makes Mandil different from a ride-hailing app?+

Certainty. A confirmed chauffeur assigned hours in advance rather than matched at the kerb; a fixed fare rather than surge pricing; flight tracking, meet & greet and luggage service rather than a pin on a map. It's the difference between a ride and a welcome.
