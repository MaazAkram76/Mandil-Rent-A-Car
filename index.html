    Good to know

    Questions, answered.

    How far in advance should I book?+

    We recommend a few hours ahead for airport transfers, though we happily accommodate last-minute requests subject to availability. For tours and corporate contracts, earlier is better.

    What happens if my flight is delayed?+

    Nothing on your side. We track your flight in real time and adjust the pickup automatically, at no extra cost. Your chauffeur will be there when you land.

    Are your prices fixed or metered?+

    Every fare is fixed and confirmed in writing before you travel. No surge pricing, no detour charges, no surprises.

    Do you offer chauffeured and self-drive rentals?+

    Both. Choose a professional chauffeur or drive yourself, by the day or the month, whichever suits your journey.

    Which payment methods do you accept?+

    Cash, bank transfer and major cards. Corporate clients can be set up on monthly invoicing.
