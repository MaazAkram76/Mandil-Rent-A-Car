Questions, answered

Before you take the keys.

What do I need to rent a car?+

A valid driver's licence, your CNIC or passport, and a refundable security deposit. For self-drive we complete simple verification at booking, it takes minutes, not meetings.

Is there a security deposit?+

Yes, a refundable deposit that varies by vehicle class. It is documented at handover and returned promptly at the end of the rental once the vehicle is checked.

Can foreigners rent with an international licence?+

Yes. A valid international driving permit alongside your passport is accepted for self-drive. Many international visitors prefer adding a chauffeur, the cost difference is smaller than you'd expect.

What is the fuel policy?+

Simple and fair: the car goes out with a documented fuel level and comes back at the same level. No pre-purchase schemes, no refuelling penalties.

Is there a mileage limit?+

Standard rentals include generous daily mileage that suits normal city and intercity use. Planning serious distance? Tell us at booking and we'll agree terms up front, never at return.

Is insurance included?+

All vehicles carry comprehensive cover. Your responsibilities as the driver are explained plainly at handover, in writing, before you sign anything.

Can I add a second driver?+

Yes, additional drivers can be added at booking with the same simple verification. For long trips we recommend it.

Do you deliver the car?+

Across Islamabad and Rawalpindi, yes, to your home, office, hotel or the airport. The delivery includes a walk-around and handover briefing wherever you are.

What if I return the car late?+

Life happens, message us. Short grace periods are handled with common sense, and longer overruns simply convert to the agreed daily rate. No punitive fees.

Can I extend my rental?+

Almost always, with one message, most extensions are confirmed within the hour, subject to the vehicle's next booking. Extending early keeps your same car.

How do I pay?+

Cash, bank transfer or major cards. Corporate clients can move to monthly invoicing through a corporate account.

Do you offer corporate or fleet rentals?+

Yes, pool cars, visiting-executive vehicles and project fleets with consolidated billing and a dedicated account manager. Start with our corporate transportation page.

Self-drive or chauffeur, how do I decide?+

Self-drive suits daily flexibility and familiar roads. A chauffeur earns their keep on long distances, dense schedules, weddings and unfamiliar cities. You can also mix: self-drive for the week, chauffeur for the big day.

Can I take the car to another city?+

Yes, intercity use is welcome on most vehicles; just tell us your route at booking so we set mileage terms and recommend the right car for the road surface and season.

Can I take a rental to the northern areas?+

For the high north we recommend our 4x4s, and honestly, our northern tours with a mountain-experienced driver. Self-drive north is possible on selected vehicles with prior agreement.

What condition are the cars in?+

Recent models, professionally serviced, inspected and detailed before each rental. You receive the car in the condition you'd keep your own, we ask only the same in return.

What happens if the car breaks down?+

Call us, any hour. We arrange assistance immediately and, where needed, a replacement vehicle so your plans survive intact. Breakdowns are rare; our response isn't.

Can I book a car for someone else?+

Yes, parents, guests, visiting colleagues. The driver completes verification; you handle the booking and, if you like, the bill.

What is the cancellation policy?+

Free cancellation with reasonable notice; terms are stated clearly on your confirmation. We'd rather you rebook with confidence than hesitate to book at all.

How far in advance should I book?+

Popular vehicles, Fortuner, Prado, the luxury sedans, go early for weekends and wedding season. A few days' notice usually secures your first choice; same-day is often possible for the wider fleet.
