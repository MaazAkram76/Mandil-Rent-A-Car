The first impression

Some meetings are decided before anyone sits down.

Watch an experienced executive arrive somewhere important. They step out of the car unhurried. No searching for parking, no glancing at a phone, no small frustrations carried through the door. The day has been travelling in their favour since the morning — and it shows in the first handshake.

That composure is not a personality trait. It is logistics, done invisibly well. When the driving, the routing, the timing and the waiting are all someone else's profession, your attention stays where it earns its keep: the brief on your lap, the call in progress, or simply a quiet moment before the room fills.

Mandil's executive chauffeur service exists for the people whose days are built from such moments — executives, diplomats, hosts of important guests. One professional at the wheel. One standard throughout. And a version of the city where everything is simply handled.

Questions, answered

The particulars.

How do I book a chauffeur?+

Online, by WhatsApp or by phone — personally or through your office. Share the date, hours and first pickup point; we confirm the chauffeur, vehicle and fixed rate in writing.

Can I hire by the hour?+

Yes. Hourly hire suits single meetings and short engagements; half-day and full-day rates reward longer bookings. The chauffeur and car remain exclusively yours throughout.

What does a full-day booking include?+

A dedicated chauffeur and vehicle at your disposal for the day — unlimited stops, waiting included, itinerary changes welcome. The car is wherever you need it, whenever you emerge.

How discreet is the service, really?+

Completely. Chauffeurs are bound to confidentiality, itineraries are shared on a need-to-know basis, and nothing about your travel is discussed with anyone. We serve embassies and boardrooms on precisely this basis.

Can my company open an account?+

Yes — corporate accounts bring priority booking, consolidated monthly invoicing and a dedicated manager. Details on our corporate transportation page.

Do you handle airport pickups for executives?+

Routinely. Flights are tracked, the chauffeur waits at arrivals with a name board, and the journey continues straight into the day's schedule. See airport transfers for the full service.

Can hotels book on behalf of guests?+

Yes — concierge teams book with us daily. The guest receives chauffeur details directly; the hotel receives confirmation and consolidated billing if preferred.

Will the chauffeur wait during my meetings?+

Of course — waiting is part of the engagement, not an extra. However long the meeting runs, the car is at the door when you step out.

Can I request the same chauffeur each time?+

Yes. Regular clients often build a preference, and we honour it wherever schedules allow. Familiarity is part of good service.

Which vehicles can I choose from?+

Mercedes-Benz E-Class, BMW 5 Series, Lexus LX 570, Land Cruiser, Prado and other executive vehicles. Tell us the occasion; we'll recommend the right cabin.

Do you serve weddings and special occasions?+

Yes — bridal cars, family VIP movements and guest logistics, with vehicles presented immaculately and chauffeurs briefed on the day's choreography.

Is the service available at night?+

Around the clock. Late dinners, dawn flights, overnight standby during critical visits — the standard holds at every hour.

How is pricing structured?+

By the hour, the day or the engagement — always fixed and confirmed in advance. No meters, no overtime ambushes; extended hours convert at the agreed rate.

How do I pay?+

Cash, transfer or card for individual bookings; monthly invoicing on corporate accounts. Receipts and trip summaries are provided as standard.

What if my schedule changes mid-day?+

Tell the chauffeur — that's all. New addresses, reordered meetings and added stops are absorbed without ceremony.

What is the cancellation policy?+

Flexible with reasonable notice, and stated plainly on your confirmation. Rescheduling is always the easier conversation — and we make it easy.

Are the chauffeurs vetted?+

Thoroughly: identity, licensing, driving record and references, followed by our own training in executive service and defensive driving. We hire slowly on purpose.

Can you manage multi-vehicle movements?+

Yes — delegations, board visits and event convoys are coordinated centrally with a single point of contact and synchronised timings.

Do you operate outside Islamabad?+

Full-day engagements travel wherever you do — Lahore, Peshawar and beyond. For dedicated long-distance journeys, see intercity travel.

How is this different from ride-hailing's premium tiers?+

A named, vetted professional assigned in advance; a vehicle prepared for you alone; waiting, discretion and continuity built in. Ride-hailing sells trips. This is a service relationship.
