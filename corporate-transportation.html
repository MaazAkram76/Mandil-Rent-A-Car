Questions, answered

For your operations team.

How do we open a corporate account?+

Request a consultation. We assess your mobility needs, present a proposal with agreed rates, and set up your account, booking channels, billing and account manager, typically within days.

Is there a minimum monthly commitment?+

No rigid minimums. Accounts range from occasional executive bookings to dedicated daily fleets; the contract is shaped around your actual usage.

How does monthly billing work?+

All trips consolidate into one monthly invoice with per-trip detail, date, route, traveller reference and cost centre if you use them. Supporting documentation is standard.

Can we allocate costs by department?+

Yes, bookings can carry department or project codes, and invoices can be split or subtotalled accordingly.

Who can make bookings on our account?+

You define the authorised bookers, admins, EAs, HR or department heads, and we honour only those channels. Changes take one email from your account owner.

How fast can you fulfil a same-day request?+

Account clients hold priority on the fleet. Most same-day requests in the twin cities are confirmed within the hour, subject to vehicle class.

Can we have dedicated vehicles and chauffeurs?+

Yes, long-term contracts can embed specific vehicles and named chauffeurs in your operation, exclusively yours for the contract's duration.

Do you handle employee shift transportation?+

Yes, scheduled staff movements with fixed routes, attendance discipline and vehicles sized to your teams, including Hiace and Coaster options.

Can you manage our visiting executives end-to-end?+

That's a signature use case: airport pickup with flight tracking, hotel transfers, daily chauffeured schedules and final departure, one itinerary, one point of contact.

Do you serve conferences and corporate events?+

Yes, multi-vehicle logistics for speakers, delegates and VIPs, with a movement plan agreed in advance and a coordinator on call throughout.

What about embassies and international organisations?+

We serve missions and international bodies with protocol-aware chauffeurs, documented vehicles and the discretion such work requires. References available on request.

Are your vehicles and drivers documented for procurement?+

Fully, registration, insurance, maintenance records and driver vetting files are maintained and available for compliance and procurement review.

What are your business hours?+

Operations run 24/7, year-round. Account management keeps office hours; the support line never closes.

Which vehicles are available under contract?+

The full Mandil fleet, executive sedans (Mercedes, BMW), luxury SUVs (Lexus, Land Cruiser, Prado, Fortuner), business sedans and group vehicles, allocated by your programme's needs.

Can special requests be accommodated?+

Routinely, specific vehicles, preferred chauffeurs, signage-free discretion, child seats for relocating families, or standby cover during critical visits. Ask your account manager.

What payment methods do you accept?+

Bank transfer against monthly invoices is standard for accounts; cards and other arrangements can be agreed in your contract.

How flexible are the contracts?+

Deliberately flexible: scale up for launches and audit seasons, scale down in quiet quarters. Terms are reviewed with you, not imposed on you.

What happens in an emergency or late change?+

Call the 24/7 line. Vehicles are redeployed, backups assigned and your coordinator kept informed until the movement completes. Contingency is part of the service.

Which service areas are covered?+

Islamabad and Rawalpindi comprehensively, with intercity corporate travel nationwide through intercity travel and retreat programmes via northern tours.

Why Mandil over managing this in-house?+

Owning vehicles means owning maintenance, drivers, insurance, scheduling and liability. A partner converts all of it into one predictable line item, with a better fleet than most companies would buy themselves.
